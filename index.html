    About Smart Kitchen

    Smart Kitchen is a web-based platform designed to make cooking easier, faster, and more efficient.

    It helps users discover recipes based on the ingredients they already have at home, reducing food waste and saving time.

    Ingredient-Based Suggestions: Enter the ingredients you have, and get instant recipe ideas.
    Famous Recipes: Explore popular and easy-to-cook dishes from different cuisines.
    Healthy Cooking: Learn to cook delicious meals while maintaining a healthy diet.
    User-Friendly Interface: Simple and intuitive design suitable for beginners and experienced cooks alike.

    Smart Kitchen aims to empower everyone in the kitchen, minimize food waste, and make cooking a fun and stress-free experience.
